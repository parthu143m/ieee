What is IEE

IEEE, pronounced "Eye-triple-E," stands for the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. It is a professional association that aims to foster technological innovation and excellence. IEEE is the largest technical professional organization in the world, with over 400,000 members in more than 160 countries.
